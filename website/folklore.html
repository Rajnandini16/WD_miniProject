Folklore

Folklore is the collection of traditional stories, customs, beliefs, and artistic expressions shared within a community and passed down informally through generations. It's the everyday culture of a group, encompassing tales told around a fire, traditional practices at festivals, age-old superstitions, and the unique ways a community expresses its identity and values through oral tradition, customs, material objects, and performances. It acts as a cultural glue, connecting people to their past and to each other.

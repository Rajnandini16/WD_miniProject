Myths

Myths are old stories that people used to tell to explain things like how the world was made, why the sun shines, or why people do certain things. These stories often have gods, magical creatures, or heroes in them.

Mythological stories are the stories from myths. They are not usually real, but people believed them a long time ago. They helped explain things before science existed and also taught lessons about right and wrong.
